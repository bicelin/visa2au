Contact Visa2AU

Every engagement starts with a conversation. A consultation lets us better understand your situation — clearly identify the issue and strategise the right pathways for you.

What a professional consultation gives you

Tailored research

We research your specific circumstances, visa history and eligibility under current immigration law — before the call.

Fair assessment

An honest professional evaluation of your situation — strengths, risks and potential challenges included.

Realistic prospects

Clear guidance on your chances of success, based on legislation, policy and our case experience.

Right strategy

A personalised roadmap: the best pathway, timeline, and the exact steps to maximise your chances.

How we can meet

Video consultation

Online video call via our secure internal platform. Most popular and convenient. Sessions may be recorded for compliance purposes.

Phone consultation

Audio-only consultation via phone or voice-call apps — suitable if you prefer voice-only communication.

Email consultation

Written consultation via email exchange. Ideal for complex cases requiring detailed written analysis.
