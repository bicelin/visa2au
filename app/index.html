Quick answers

How much does it cost to use a migration agent? +

Consultations are 330 AUD per hour (pro rata applies). Most visa matters are quoted as a fixed professional fee before you commit — government charges are shown separately on our pricing page, including indicative professional fees for common visa types.

How long does an Australian visa take? +

Processing varies widely: a Visitor visa can take days to weeks, partner visas commonly run 12–24 months, and skilled visas depend on invitation rounds. Each visa guide on this site shows current indicative processing times from the Department of Home Affairs.

Can you guarantee my visa will be granted? +

No — and you should be wary of anyone who does. Only the Department of Home Affairs decides applications. What we guarantee is a legally sound, complete and honest application: our 99.8% success rate across 2,000+ cases reflects rigorous screening before we lodge.

Do I need to be in Australia to apply? +

Not necessarily. Many visas — including skilled, partner (offshore 309/100) and visitor visas — are lodged from overseas. We work with clients in 40+ countries via video consultation, in English, Russian, French and 24 other languages.
