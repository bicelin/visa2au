# Protection Visa (subclass 866) — Visa2AU

> Protection claims handled with care and confidentiality. Category: Other.

Protection visas (866) are for people in Australia who fear persecution or significant harm at home. These matters demand meticulous evidence, confidentiality and realistic advice about prospects and alternatives.

## At a glance
- Government charge: From AUD 45
- Indicative professional fee: $5,500 AUD (Confidential consultation first)
- Typical processing: Varies widely; often 12+ months

## Eligibility
- In Australia and not barred from applying
- Well-founded fear of persecution (refugee criteria) or real risk of significant harm (complementary protection)
- Meet identity, health and character requirements

## Key documents
- Identity documents
- Detailed statement of claims
- Country information and supporting evidence
- Any witness or documentary corroboration

## Frequently asked questions
### Will my family overseas be told about my claim?
No. Protection applications are confidential and the Department does not contact your home country authorities about your claims.
