# Skills Assessments (subclass VETASSESS · TRA · EA · ACS) — Visa2AU

> Get your qualifications recognised for skilled migration. Category: Skilled.

A positive skills assessment is the gateway to skilled and employer-sponsored visas. Each assessing authority (VETASSESS, Trades Recognition Australia, Engineers Australia, ACS, ANMAC and others) has its own evidence rules — getting it right the first time avoids months of delay.

## At a glance
- Government charge: AUD 500–3,000+ (authority dependent)
- Indicative professional fee: $3,300 AUD (Fixed professional fee per application)
- Typical processing: 8–16 weeks (authority dependent)

## Eligibility
- Qualifications comparable to the Australian standard for your ANZSCO occupation
- Required years of post-qualification employment
- Occupation-specific evidence (e.g. employment references, CDR for engineers, OSAP for some trades)

## Key documents
- Qualification certificates and transcripts
- Detailed employment references (duties mapped to ANZSCO)
- Payslips, tax records, contracts as employment proof
- Licences or registrations where held
- English evidence where the authority requires it

## Frequently asked questions
### Which assessing authority handles my occupation?
It depends on the ANZSCO code: VETASSESS covers most professional and general occupations, TRA handles trades (with pathways like OSAP and MSA), Engineers Australia assesses engineering, and the ACS covers ICT. Choosing the right code is as important as the evidence itself.
