# Short Stay Specialist (subclass 400/408) — Visa2AU

> Highly specialised short-term work. Category: Visitor.

The 400 (Temporary Work — Short Stay Specialist) and 408 (Temporary Activity) visas cover short, highly specialised assignments, events and activities that do not fit standard sponsorship.

## At a glance
- Government charge: From AUD 415 (400) / AUD 415 (408)
- Indicative professional fee: $3,300 AUD (Fixed professional fee per application)
- Typical processing: 2–8 weeks (stream dependent)

## Eligibility
- Highly specialised skills, knowledge or experience not readily available in Australia
- Genuine short-term purpose (generally up to 3 months, 6 in strong cases)
- Invitation or support from an Australian organisation
- Meet health and character requirements

## Key documents
- Invitation/support letters
- Evidence of specialised expertise
- Itinerary and project details
- Financial evidence

## Frequently asked questions
### When is a 400 better than a 482?
For genuinely short, specialised assignments where full sponsorship is disproportionate — equipment installation, expert assessments, one-off projects. It cannot be used to fill ongoing positions.
