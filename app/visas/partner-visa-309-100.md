# Partner Visa (Offshore) (subclass 309/100) — Visa2AU

> Apply from outside Australia, then settle permanently. Category: Family.

The offshore partner pathway (309 temporary, then 100 permanent) suits couples where the applicant is outside Australia. It mirrors the onshore 820/801 two-stage process with the same relationship-evidence standard.

## At a glance
- Government charge: From AUD 11,710 (main applicant)
- Indicative professional fee: $5,500 AUD (Fixed professional fee quoted after assessment)
- Typical processing: 12–24 months (typical)

## Eligibility
- Outside Australia at time of application and (usually) decision of 309
- Genuine spouse or de facto relationship with eligible sponsor
- Sponsor is an Australian citizen, PR or eligible NZ citizen
- Meet health and character requirements

## Key documents
- Identity and civil status documents
- Evidence of genuine relationship across financial, household, social and commitment aspects
- Form 888 statutory declarations
- Police clearances from all relevant countries
- Health examinations

## Frequently asked questions
### Can I visit Australia while my 309 is processing?
Yes, many applicants visit on a visitor visa while waiting, but the visitor visa has its own genuine-temporary-entrant test. We plan the timing carefully to avoid refusals that complicate your history.

### Is the 309/100 fee the same as 820/801?
The government application charge is the same for onshore and offshore partner streams. Secondary applicant charges and biometrics/health costs differ by country.
