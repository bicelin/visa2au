# Bridging Visas (subclass BVA–BVE) — Visa2AU

> Keep your status lawful between visas. Category: Other.

Bridging visas keep you lawful while a substantive application or review is decided. Work rights, travel (BVB) and conditions vary — managing them correctly prevents accidental unlawful status.

## At a glance
- Government charge: No charge (linked grant)
- Indicative professional fee: From $1,100 AUD (Advice typically within a consultation)
- Typical processing: Usually granted automatically with the linked application

## Eligibility
- Linked to a pending substantive visa application or ART review
- Conditions depend on your previous visa and circumstances

## Key documents
- ImmiAccount records
- BVB application if travel is needed
- Evidence for work-rights variation (financial hardship cases)

## Frequently asked questions
### Can I travel while my application is pending?
Only on a Bridging Visa B — and you must hold it before you leave. Departing on a BVA cancels it and can strand you offshore; always confirm the BVB grant first.
