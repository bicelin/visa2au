# Training Visa (subclass 407) — Visa2AU

> Structured workplace training and professional development. Category: Employer.

The 407 visa supports occupational training and professional development in Australia under an approved sponsor. It suits employers building skills (e.g. cooks, cabinetmakers, managers) through structured training plans.

## At a glance
- Government charge: From AUD 415
- Indicative professional fee: $3,300 AUD (Sponsorship + nomination + application fees apply)
- Typical processing: 3–6 months (typical)

## Eligibility
- Approved temporary activities sponsor
- Nomination with a structured training plan
- Genuine intention to complete the training
- Relevant background for the training occupation
- Meet health, character and insurance requirements

## Key documents
- Sponsorship and nomination approvals
- Detailed training plan (objectives, supervision, assessment)
- CV and qualifications
- Employment evidence
- Health insurance

## Frequently asked questions
### How long can a 407 visa be granted for?
Up to two years, matching the length of the approved training program. It is not a work visa — the training plan controls what the visa holder does.
