# Work & Holiday Visas (subclass 417/462) — Visa2AU

> Work and travel Australia for young adults. Category: Visitor.

Working Holiday (417) and Work and Holiday (462) visas let young adults from eligible countries holiday in Australia while funding their trip with short-term work. Second and third year extensions are available with specified work.

## At a glance
- Government charge: From AUD 650
- Indicative professional fee: $2,200 AUD (Fixed professional fee per application)
- Typical processing: 2–6 weeks (typical)

## Eligibility
- Passport from an eligible country (417 or 462 list)
- Aged 18–30 (35 for some countries)
- Sufficient funds (approx. AUD 5,000 plus fare)
- For 462: education and English requirements, plus letter of support where applicable

## Key documents
- Passport
- Bank statements
- Education evidence (462)
- English test results (462, where required)

## Frequently asked questions
### How do I get a second year Working Holiday visa?
Complete three months (88 days) of specified work — typically regional work in agriculture, construction, tourism or bushfire recovery — while on your first 417/462 visa, with payslips as evidence.
