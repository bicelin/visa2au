# Visitor Visa (subclass 600) — Visa2AU

> Tourist and business visitor streams. Category: Visitor.

The subclass 600 covers tourism, family visits and short business trips. The key to approval is demonstrating genuine temporary stay: ties to home, funds, and a credible itinerary.

## At a glance
- Government charge: From AUD 250 (offshore)
- Indicative professional fee: $700 AUD (Fixed professional fee per application)
- Typical processing: 2–4 weeks (typical)

## Eligibility
- Genuine intention to stay temporarily
- Adequate funds for the trip
- Meet health and character requirements
- Strong incentive to return home (employment, family, assets)

## Key documents
- Passport and identity documents
- Bank statements and employment evidence
- Itinerary and accommodation details
- Invitation letter (if visiting family/friends)

## Frequently asked questions
### Why are visitor visas refused?
Most refusals cite insufficient evidence of genuine temporary stay — weak home ties, inconsistent finances, or unclear purpose. A well-prepared application addresses these upfront.

### How long can I stay on a 600 visa?
Stays of 3, 6 or 12 months may be granted depending on the stream and your circumstances. We request the period that matches your itinerary.
