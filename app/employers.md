# Employer sponsorship services — Visa2AU

> End-to-end employer-sponsored migration: Standard Business Sponsorship (SBS), nominations, Skills in Demand 482, Skilled Regional 494, Employer Nomination 186, and DAMA labour agreements.

## Streams
- Skills in Demand (482): Specialist Skills (salary from AUD 146,717), Core Skills (CSOL occupations, salary from AUD 79,499), Labour Agreement (incl. DAMA).
- Employer Nomination Scheme (186): Direct Entry and Temporary Residence Transition (after 2 years with sponsor on 482).
- Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (494): 5-year regional visa, permanent 191 pathway after 3 years.

## DAMA
Designated Area Migration Agreements give regional employers concessions on age, English, salary and occupation lists. We advise on DAMA regions (e.g. Cairns, Far North Queensland; Northern Territory; South Australia; Goldfields WA; Orana NSW and others), negotiate variations and manage endorsements.

## Employer obligations
Sponsorship obligations include record-keeping, equivalent terms and conditions, non-discrimination in recruitment, paying nomination SAF levy, and notifying the Department of changes.

## Process
1. Business case and eligibility review
2. SBS accreditation / renewal
3. Nomination with Labour Market Testing evidence
4. Visa application and decision management
