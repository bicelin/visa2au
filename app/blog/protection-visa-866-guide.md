The Protection Visa (subclass 866) is for people who are in Australia and engage Australia’s protection obligations. This visa allows you to live and work in Australia as a permanent resident.

## Who Can Apply?

You may be eligible for a Protection Visa if you:

- Are in Australia (lawfully or unlawfully)
- Owe protection obligations to Australia
- Meet health, security, and character requirements
- Have not held certain visas previously

## What Does Protection Mean?

Australia owes you protection if you are a refugee or meet the complementary protection criteria:

**Refugee Criteria:**
You have a well-founded fear of persecution in your home country due to race, religion, nationality, membership of a particular social group, or political opinion.

**Complementary Protection:**
There is a real risk you will suffer significant harm if returned to your home country.

## Application Process

The process involves a Protection Visa Application, followed by an interview with a departmental officer. The department will assess your claims and make a decision.

## Rights as a Protection Visa Holder

- Live and work in Australia permanently
- Access Medicare
- Sponsor eligible family members
- Travel to and from Australia for 5 years
- Apply for Australian citizenship when eligible
