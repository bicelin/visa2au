The Partner Visa (subclass 820/801) is one of the most common pathways for couples where one partner is an Australian citizen, permanent resident, or eligible New Zealand citizen. This visa allows the partner to live in Australia temporarily (subclass 820) and later transition to permanent residency (subclass 801).

## The Two-Stage Process

**Stage 1: Temporary Partner Visa (Subclass 820)**
This is the first stage of the application. It allows you to remain in Australia while your permanent visa application is processed. You can work, study, and access Medicare during this period.

**Stage 2: Permanent Partner Visa (Subclass 801)**
Approximately two years after lodging your initial application, you may be eligible for the permanent visa if your relationship is ongoing and genuine.

## Key Requirements

- Be in a genuine and continuing relationship with an Australian citizen, permanent resident, or eligible NZ citizen
- Meet health and character requirements
- Provide substantial evidence of your relationship across four categories: financial, domestic, social, and commitment
- Be in Australia when applying (for subclass 820)

## Processing Times

Processing times vary significantly based on individual circumstances. As of 2024, most applications are processed within 12-24 months for the temporary visa.
