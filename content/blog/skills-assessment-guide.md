For most skilled visa pathways to Australia, you will need a positive skills assessment from the relevant assessing authority. This is a critical step that must be completed before lodging your Expression of Interest (EOI).

## What is a Skills Assessment?

A skills assessment evaluates whether your qualifications and work experience meet Australian standards for your nominated occupation. Each occupation has a designated assessing authority.

## Major Assessing Authorities

- **VETASSESS** - Trades and professional occupations
- **ACS** - IT and computing professionals
- **Engineers Australia** - Engineering occupations
- **CPA/CA/IPA** - Accounting professionals
- **ANMAC** - Nurses and midwives
- **TRA** - Trade occupations
- **AIM** - Management occupations

## The Assessment Process

1. **Check Requirements** - Visit the assessing authority's website to understand specific requirements for your occupation
2. **Prepare Documents** - Gather qualifications, employment references, and supporting evidence
3. **Lodge Application** - Submit your application online with the required fee
4. **Await Outcome** - Processing times vary from 4 weeks to 6 months

## Tips for a Successful Assessment

- Ensure your employment references include all required details (dates, duties, hours)
- Have your qualifications assessed for Australian equivalence if obtained overseas
- Allow plenty of time - skills assessments can take several months
